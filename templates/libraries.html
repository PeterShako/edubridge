All public libraries in Kenya are managed by the Kenya National Library Service (KNLS), a statutory body of the Government of Kenya established in 1965 by an Act of Parliament, Cap 225 of the laws of Kenya. The KNLS is mandated to “promote, establish, equip, manage, maintain and develop libraries in Kenya.”

Libraries play a vital role in providing a serene environment where people can research and get valuable information to quench their thirst. They have amazing collections of books, creatively written to suit readers. Their inventories also have old newspapers dating back a few centuries.

Public Libraries in Nairobi County

1. Kenya National Library Service (KNLS)

Location: Upper Hill, opposite NHIF Building.

Services: Reading facilities, book lending, Internet and bureau services, Braille library services, mobile library services, and more.

Entrance Fees: Daily Ksh 20 for adults, free for children. Membership: Registration fee Ksh 300, Annual fee Ksh 100.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 8 am to 6.30 pm, Saturday: 8.30 am to 5 pm. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Contact: Phone: +254 202739893/2739894/2739896

2. KNLS Buruburu Public Library

Location: Buruburu Phase 2, along Mumias South Road.

Services: Reading facilities, book lending, Internet and bureau services, Braille library services, and more.

Charges: Registration fee Ksh 300, Annual fee Ksh 100.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 8 am to 6.30 pm, Saturday: 8.30 am to 5 pm. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Email: publiclibrary@knls.ac.ke

3. McMillan Memorial Library

Location: Banda Street, next to Jamia Mosque.

Services: Reading facilities, book lending, Africana section, and periodical section.

Charges: Annual subscription: Adults Ksh 100, Children Ksh 50. Researchers: Ksh 200 a week.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 8 am to 6 pm. Closed on Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays.

Contact: Phone: +254 020 721 2651

4. Rahimtulla Trust Library

Location: Mfangano Street, near the Central Bus Station.

Services: Reading facilities, book lending, and periodicals section.

Charges: Daily Ksh 30, Weekly Ksh 150, Monthly Ksh 500, Annual Ksh 4000.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 8 am to 6 pm. Closed on Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays.

Contact: Phone: +254 (0)20 2312822

5. Kenya National Archives

Location: Moi Avenue, opposite the Hilton Hotel.

Services: Reading facilities, book lending, rare African books, and public documents access.

Charges: Annual membership Ksh 200.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 8.30 am to 4.30 pm, Saturday: 8.30 am to 1 pm. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Email: info@kenyarchives.go.ke

6. East African Herbarium Library

Location: Nairobi National Museum.

Services: Reading facilities, book and journal lending, and botanical collections access.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday: 8 am to 5 pm.

Contact: Phone: +254 20 4448930

7. Jomo Kenyatta Memorial Library

Location: University of Nairobi main campus, off University Way.

Services: Reading facilities, book lending, Internet and bureau services, binding services, university theses, and periodicals.

Charges: Reading only: Daily Ksh 100, Monthly Ksh 500. External borrowers: Ksh 500 plus a refundable deposit of Ksh 3000.

Opening Hours: During session: Monday to Friday 8 am to 10 pm, Saturday 8 am to 5 pm. During vacation: Monday to Friday 8 am to 5 pm, Saturday 8 am to noon. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Email: librarian@uonbi.ac.ke
